    About New Horizons Learning Methods



    Are you looking to add new technical skills? Are you a corporate trainer or manager who wants to broaden your employees’ knowledge? New Horizons South Florida’s Integrated Learning solution provides you with the efficient and cost effective training you need.






    Instructor-Led Training



    The Classroom Experience

    New Horizons South Florida’s Instructor-led training provides you with an interactive and engaging classroom experience blending:

  • Lectures
  • Demonstrations
  • Hands-on practice
  • Valuable student and peer interaction
  • Online resources to use during and after class

    • As a classroom student, New Horizons South Florida gives you access to your own networked environment, giving you professional, hands-on experience.

    The technology industry’s most experienced and qualified instructors will provide you with the highest-quality training, ensuring you can immediately apply the course’s processes, concepts, and methodologies to your job.








    Online ANYTIME Training



    Set Your Own Online Training Schedule And Pace

    Do you need training, but have a busy schedule and want to work at your own pace? Online ANYTIME training at New Horizons South Florida provides a convenient and flexible eLearning option.

    Available as a web-based and computer-based training option, Online ANYTIME allows you to access your courses at home, from the office, or on the road anytime, day or night, without any investment in servers, licensing, or software.

    If you prefer an independent learning approach, Online ANYTIME is a great option. New Horizons South Florida designs each course to include a definition of learning objectives, instructional strategies, and appropriate learning activities.

    Broken down into searchable modules, Online ANYTIME allows you to easily find different parts of the course on which to focus.



    HANDS-ON TRAINING ANYTIME, ANYWHERE

    Just as with News Horizons South Florida’s traditional classroom courses, Online ANYTIME includes the same core content. Its interactive learning experience also integrates sophisticated tools and techniques, all while staying easy to use.

    You can measure how well you absorb the different learning objectives through practice questions and assessments. The self-paced environments and unlimited access to course information give you the opportunity to hone your new skills.

    Self-paced doesn’t mean hands-off. New Horizons South Florida’s vLab gives you hands-on experience during the course, and connects you to actual hardware and software. You can also use the vLab to practice and reinforce your new skills. Through Online ANYTIME, you will gain real world experience to ready you for future job tasks.







    Online Live Training



    Online LIVE Provides the Same Great Learning Experience as the Classroom

    Online LIVE training provides the same high-quality education, including live lectures, demonstrations and virtual labs, as a traditional classroom training, all while gaining flexibility.

    Online LIVE training allows you to:

  • Participate in discussions via live video and audio feeds
  • Perform virtual labs for hands-on experience
  • View documents and presentations in real time
  • Play back any class for review
  • Attend virtual rooms to facilitate team activities
  • Participate in discussions with your instructor and fellow students
  • Online Live Training at New Horizons South Florida
  • With New Horizons South Florida’s remote sharing technology, instructors can even view and manage the student’s computer for one-to-one mentoring.


    • Comprehensive Learning Environment

    New Horizons South Florida’s Online LIVE platform has minimal technical requirements and is easy to operate, giving you access to training from home, the office, or even when you are on the road.

    In addition, New Horizons South Florida gives you access to an OLL learning lab where you can view your courses, ensuring your success. You will also receive on-site learning support, providing you the opportunity to have a focused environment without distractions if you choose.

    Online LIVE also gives you access to the client and server technologies covered in class via New Horizons’ Labs on Demand. This means you have remote access to the technology you are learning to test and apply your new skills on real equipment. You also have the flexibility to access labs before and after actual class hours, providing you with ample time to gain extra experience.
